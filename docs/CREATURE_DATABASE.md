## Lore entries

**#1 Sproutling** — *Origin Village*

> A cheerful seed-sprite that sleeps buried to its chin in warm soil. Its leaf quivers when rain is coming.

**#2 Florabeast** — *Verdant Forest*

> Its petals open only in battle. The pollen it scatters makes opponents drowsy and dreamful.

**#3 Elderbloom** — *Sky Temple gardens*

> A walking garden centuries old. Wounded creatures that rest beneath its canopy wake fully healed.

**#4 Cindercub** — *Origin Village*

> A soot-furred cub that sneezes sparks when excited. It hoards warm pebbles in its cheeks.

**#5 Pyrelynx** — *Desert Frontier*

> Its whiskers read heat the way eyes read light. It stalks prey through total darkness by warmth alone.

**#6 Solfang** — *Thunder Plateau*

> Legends say its fangs are shards of a fallen sun. Its roar turns night to noon for a single heartbeat.

**#7 Dripple** — *Origin Village*

> A droplet-shaped spirit that polishes river stones for fun. It cries when ponds dry up — refilling them.

**#8 Marlune** — *Crystal Caverns pools*

> Its crescent fin glows with moonlight it drank as a Dripple. Tides shift subtly wherever it swims.

**#9 Leviadon** — *Deep lakes*

> A gentle titan whose shell hosts whole reefs. When it surfaces, fishermen swear the horizon bends.

**#10 Chirpuff** — *Verdant Forest canopy*

> A palm-sized puffball that rides breezes it whistles into being. It naps inside its own tiny whirlwind.

**#11 Galehawk** — *Open skies*

> It folds its wings and falls like a javelin. Listeners on the ground hear the sky tear.

**#12 Tempestrix** — *Thunder Plateau*

> Storm queen of the high plateau. Lightning courses harmlessly through her feathers — then not so harmlessly through her foes.

**#13 Mossling** — *Verdant Forest floor*

> Indistinguishable from a mossy stone until it yawns. Hikers apologize to rocks out of habit.

**#14 Thornhide** — *Verdant Forest deep groves*

> Its back is a fortress of living briars. Forest folk leave offerings of rainwater at its sleeping spots.

**#15 Glowbug** — *Verdant Forest at night*

> Its abdomen stores static from rubbing against flower petals. Swarms light the forest like festival lanterns.

**#16 Voltmoth** — *Thunder Plateau*

> Its wing scales are natural capacitors. A resting Voltmoth can power a village mill for a day.

**#17 Pebblit** — *Crystal Caverns*

> A round stone-child that tumbles instead of walking. It collects shiny crystals it can never carry home.

**#18 Boulderon** — *Crystal Caverns depths*

> It naps for years at a time, waking only when miners' songs annoy it. Avalanches follow its tantrums.

**#19 Titanstone** — *Mountain hearts*

> Mountains are said to be Titanstones that never woke up. The crystals on its back chart the constellations of its birth-night.

**#20 Emberimp** — *Crystal Caverns vents*

> A mischievous wisp that steals candle flames. Blacksmiths bribe it with coal to keep their forges hot.

**#21 Ashfiend** — *Burnt hollows*

> Born when an Emberimp swallows a dying fire whole. It grieves for every flame it has ever extinguished.

**#22 Aquafawn** — *Forest streams*

> A shy fawn with hooves of sea-glass. It only drinks from pools that reflect the whole sky.

**#23 Mistelk** — *Foggy clearings*

> Its antlers comb fog out of the morning air. It appears only to tamers its younger self learned to trust.

**#24 Branchling** — *Verdant Forest edge*

> A twig-spirit torn between sun and moon. The charm it is given decides which voice it follows.

**#25 Solivine** — *Sunlit groves*

> Chose the sun. Its blossoms track daylight even through stone, and its nectar cures melancholy.

**#26 Lunivine** — *Moonlit groves*

> Chose the moon. It blooms only at night, and its petals remember every dream dreamt beneath them.

**#27 Sandskip** — *Desert Frontier dunes*

> It skips across dunes like a stone across water. Caravans follow its tracks to find firm sand.

**#28 Duneraptor** — *Desert Frontier wastes*

> It surfs dune-faces on splayed talons at terrifying speed. Sandstorms are sometimes just one Duneraptor showing off.

**#29 Scorchadillo** — *Desert Frontier badlands*

> Its shell plates clack open to vent furnace heat. It rolls through campfires to recharge, scattering embers.

**#30 Cactiling** — *Desert Frontier oases*

> A waddling cactus that hugs first and apologizes later. Its flower blooms once a year — wishes made then come true, allegedly.

**#31 Saguarrior** — *Desert Frontier badlands*

> A towering cactus knight that duels sandstorms for sport. Each arm-scar marks a storm it outlasted.

**#32 Frostfox** — *Frozen Ridge*

> Its nine tail-tips trace frost patterns on windows at night. It considers this high art.

**#33 Glacielle** — *Frozen Ridge aurora fields*

> A fox of living aurora. Where it walks, snow falls upward for a moment, as if the sky missed it.

**#34 Snowpup** — *Frozen Ridge slopes*

**#35 Avalanchor** — *Frozen Ridge peaks*

**#36 Shiverwing** — *Frozen Ridge skies*

**#37 Sparkit** — *Thunder Plateau foothills*

**#38 Voltail** — *Thunder Plateau*

**#39 Fulgurex** — *Thunder Plateau summit*

**#40 Gloombat** — *Crystal Caverns shadows*

**#41 Duskwraith** — *Capital City underdistrict*

**#42 Crystalisk** — *Crystal Caverns heart*

**#45 Shadeling** — *Anywhere lights gutter*

**#46 Nyxfiend** — *Bound to its tamer*

**#47 Prismaw** — *Sky Temple spires*

**#48 Emberdrake** — *Thunder Plateau thermals*

**#49 Luxorath** — *Sky Temple apex*

**#50 Umbrageist** — *Beneath Capital City*

**#51 Embercub** — *Found only through the AR lens*
