## Boss Guardians (8)

### Vael, the Bramble Warden — Thornhide L14 (verdant-forest)

An ancient Thornhide whose briar-fortress back has never been breached. It judges tamers by how gently they walk.

- **Briar Bulwark** — Every third turn, living briars harden its Defense.
- **Warden's Resolve** — Survives the first lethal blow with 1 HP, refusing to fall.
- **Rewards:** 800g, 10 crystal shards, forest-amulet, great-orb

### Korr, the Mountain's Heart — Titanstone L20 (crystal-caverns)

The oldest Titanstone, grown into the cavern itself. The crystals on its back are the cave's beating heart.

- **Crystal Carapace** — Crystal plates regrow every third turn, raising Defense.
- **Echoing Counter** — Returns 20% of all damage taken as crystalline shrapnel.
- **Rewards:** 1200g, 15 crystal shards, cavern-lantern, ember-stone

### Sirocco, the Endless Gale — Duneraptor L26 (desert-frontier)

A Duneraptor so fast that the desert's sandstorms are merely its wake. It has never finished second.

- **Tailwind Surge** — Its Speed rises every turn as the gale builds.
- **Twin Talon** — Strikes twice per turn while above 70% HP.
- **Rewards:** 1700g, 20 crystal shards, desert-compass, ultra-orb

### Boreas, the Sleeping Avalanche — Avalanchor L32 (frozen-ridge)

An Avalanchor as old as the ridge itself. Climbers pray it stays asleep; you are about to wake it.

- **Permafrost Aura** — Each turn, creeping cold may freeze the challenger.
- **Glacial Recovery** — Once, below 30% HP, it buries itself in snow and heals 40%.
- **Rewards:** 2300g, 25 crystal shards, ridge-horn, frost-gem

### Voltaras, the Stormheart — Fulgurex L38 (thunder-plateau)

The plateau's eternal storm given will. Its judgment arrives at the speed of lightning — and so does its disappointment.

- **Static Dominion** — Each turn, ambient voltage may stun the challenger.
- **Storm's Fury** — Below half HP, its Attack surges fiercely.
- **Rewards:** 3000g, 30 crystal shards, storm-sigil, mythic-orb

### Luxorath, the First Dawn — Luxorath L44 (sky-temple)

The living memory of the world's first sunrise. Its trial is not malice but measure: are you bright enough to face the dark below?

- **Dawn's Mercy** — Once, below 30% HP, the first light restores 40% of its HP.
- **Radiant Rebuke** — Returns 20% of damage taken as searing light.
- **Rewards:** 4000g, 40 crystal shards, temple-feather, full-potion

### Nocten, the Last Seal — Duskwraith L47 (capital-city)

The Duskwraith marshal who has guarded the seal beneath the Capital for a century. It hopes, every night, that no one comes.

- **Twin Shadow** — Strikes twice per turn while above 70% HP.

### Umbrageist, the First Night — Umbrageist L50 (capital-city)

Before the first dawn, there was only it. It does not want revenge — it wants to be everything again. Stop it, Grand Tamer.
